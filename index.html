How To Wash Your Hands

If the steps in the photos aren't clear, when scrubbing the soap on your hands you must scrub

- Palm To Palm

- Between Fingers

- Back Of Hands

- Base Of Thumbs

- Back Of Fingers

- Fingernails

- Wrists

- Rinse And Wipe Dry

When To Wash Hands

Before and After

-Preparing Food

-Eating

-Attending Sick People

-Handling Contact Lens

-Specifically After

-Using The Bathroom

-Blowing nose

-Coughing Or Sneezing

-Touching common surfaces (buttons,handles,etc)
